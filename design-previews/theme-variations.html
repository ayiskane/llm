Recommendations

Keep Slate if:
  • You want a professional, corporate feel
  • Blue accents work well with your brand
  • Current design is working well
Try Zinc if:
  • You want a cleaner, more minimal look
  • True neutral grays feel more modern
  • Green accents fit your use case
Try Stone if:
  • You want a warmer, softer feel
  • Less clinical, more approachable
  • Orange/amber accents work for you
Try Midnight if:
  • You want a premium, immersive feel
  • Deep blues feel more sophisticated
  • Cyan/sky accents appeal to you
